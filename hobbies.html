Hiking

"Hiking is not just a journey through mountains and forests,

but a quiet conversation with the earth each step a reminder

that the path is as important as the destination, the wind

carries stories older than we are, and the climb teaches us

that the greatest views are earned not by speed, but by patience,

persistence, and the courage to keep moving forward."

Playing Badminton

"In badminton, every serve is a promise, every rally a battle, and

every smash a declaration that speed, skill, and spirit can turn a

feathered shuttle into a soaring dream because the game isn’t just

played on the court, it’s fought in the heart."

Cooking

"Cooking is more than the act of preparing food — it’s the quiet art

of turning simple ingredients into moments of comfort, the gentle weaving

of flavors into stories, and the unspoken language of love that nourishes both the body and the soul."
